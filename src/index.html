Welcome to My Personal Website!

Hi, I'm Suryashis Ghosh, a passionate economics researcher with a keen interest in data analysis, financial forecasting, and policy evaluation. I am currently pursuing my Master of Arts in Economics at Ashoka University and have previously earned my B.Sc. in Economics from Calcutta University.

My research primarily focuses on public policy, financial markets, income inequality, and demographic trends. Over the years, I have worked on multiple projects analyzing economic growth, healthcare expenditure, and banking sector reforms. Through my work, I aim to contribute valuable insights into India's economic landscape.

Beyond research, I enjoy traveling, reading, playing chess, and mentoring students in economics and statistics. I also actively engage in social initiatives focused on education and rehabilitation programs.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my research projects, achievements, and travel experiences. You can also connect with me on LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, and other platforms linked below!
