Tungnath-Chandrashila Trip (2024)

Reaching the highest Shiva temple in the world

Unlike my previous trips with school friends, this time, I set off with my university friends for an adventurous trek to Tungnath and Chandrashila Peak. It was a different group, a different energy, and a different kind of thrill.
